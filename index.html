A Long Walk To 10 Downing Street

Mohammed Fyaz will walk with two Pakistani-born friends approximately 300 miles in 25 days while carrying a 12 foot cross on wheels with a petition for the Prime Minister. His destination is the Prime Minister's residence at 10 Downing Street in London where a small delegation from Parliament will be waiting for him to deliver the petition.

We ask you to help support this valiant effort to draw attention to the persecution faced by followers of Jesus around the world. Your donations will help to establish a specialized safe house for ex-Muslims believers in Jesus who are in danger in the UK.

We will inform you of the details of the walk once a date has been finalized.

My Human Right to Leave Islam

I call upon the UK government to review existing police and local authority policies, procedures and officers' training in regards to victims of honour violence against ex-Muslims, and to help Dostana Ministries set up a hotline to record data on hate crimes against former Muslims by Muslims. It is time for the government to take action to protect the religious freedom of those who want to leave Islam. Ex-Muslims like Nissar Hussain should not have to go into hiding in the UK.

If we are to remain a free society it is vital that everyone should be able to choose their faith, or no faith. Threats of death for those who leave Islam must be dealt with by Government and the Police.
