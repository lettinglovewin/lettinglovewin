Dostana Ministries

I created Dostana Ministries for the sake of the persecuted followers of Jesus living in Europe and around the globe. My vision is to bring the good news of Jesus Christ into every Muslim household worldwide. I also want to build a network of ex-Muslims focused on a better quality of life, fellowship, and religious freedom in the UK and abroad.

Objectives...

  • Help those fleeing Islam, forced marriages, domestic violence, rape(incest), and honor-based violence in the UK
  • Schools and mosques must tackle beliefs about honor and should play a key role in ending honor-based violence. Children must be taught gender equality, that violence against women is wrong, and the importance of Article 18 of the Human Rights Act. They must be taught that it is wrong to intimidate, threaten, or harm those who choose to leave Islam
  • Campaign for equal rights. Demand priority action through local councils and police to deal with honor based violence against former Muslims.
  • Establish a Dostana Ministries help line to gather data on the persecution of ex-Muslims and to monitor police response. We encourage police forces to recognize honor based crimes against ex-Muslims and offer support and training on how they can help the victims.
  • Keep churches and other bodies up-to-date on news of successes, tragedies, campaigns, events, and other matters impacting our communities in the UK.
  • Hold public events to promote and support fellowship, to raise better awareness of the plight of ex-Muslims, and to provide information on how churches can get involved.
  • Promote better relationships between the Muslim community and Christian organisations.
  • Raise funds to support former Muslims in the UK who are fleeing for their lives. We want to assist in relocating them to safe houses and to help them to become part of their local church or other local support network. Dostana Ministries is geared to help anyone who wants to leave Islam and who is in desperate need of assistance, whether they turn to Christ or not
  • Inform and encourage churches on how they can be a family to those fleeing Islam and how to welcome them into the body of Christ.
